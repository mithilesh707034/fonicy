About Us

Web development involves creating and maintaining websites. It includes front-end development (user interface), back-end development (server-side logic), and full-stack development (both front-end and back-end).

Integrated Development Environments (IDEs), text editors (e.g., Visual Studio Code), and browser developer tools aid in coding, debugging, and testing.

Choosing hosting services and deploying web applications to make them accessible on the internet.

Website Basics:

A website is a collection of web pages that are interconnected and typically accessible through the internet. Basic components of a website include HTML (Hypertext Markup Language), CSS (Cascading Style Sheets), and often JavaScript for interactivity.

Content Management Systems:

CMS platforms like WordPress, Joomla, or Drupal make it easier to manage and update website content without extensive coding knowledge.

E-commerce Websites:

Websites designed for online commerce allow businesses to sell products or services over the internet.
